UK HPC Training Providers

Links to providers of HPC training in the UK.

ARCHER Training The UK national supercomputing service, ARCHER, provides large amounts of HPC training that is free for all UK academics. Courses cover a range of abilities from beginner to advanced and are run at a variety of locations around the UK.

Worldwide HPC Training Providers

Training may also be available to UK HPC users from other providers around the world.

PRACE Advanced Training Centers PRACE is the pan-European HPC infrastructure and they provide advanced HPC training from a variety of centres around Europe.
